Ukugcinwa kwebhayisikili kubalulekile ekuphepheni, ekusebenzeni kahle, nasekuhlaleni isikhathi eside. Nazi izingxenye ezibalulekile:

  1. Ukuhlanza: Hlanza ibhayisikili lakho njalo ukuze uvimbele ukwakheka kokungcola okungadala ukuguguleka. Gxila ohlakeni, eketangeni, emagiyeni nasemabhrekini.
  2. Ukuthambisa: Thambisa iketango, ama-derailleur, nezindlela zamabhreki ukuze kuqinisekiswe ukusebenza kahle nokuvimbela ukugqwala. Sebenzisa isithambisi esifanele futhi ugweme ukuthambisa ngokweqile.
  3. Ukunakekelwa kwamathiya: Hlola umfutho wamathiya njalo futhi uwafuthise ku-PSI enconyiwe. Hlola amathaya ukuze ubone ukuguguleka, futhi uwathathe esikhundleni sawo uma kudingeka.
  4. Ukuhlolwa kwamabhreki: Qinisekisa ukuthi amabhreki asebenza kahle ngokuhlola ukuguguleka kwe-pad, ukuxinana kwezintambo, nokusabela kwezindlela. Lungisa noma ubeke ezinye izingxenye esikhundleni sazo uma kudingeka.
  5. Ukuguqulwa kwamagiya: Gcina amagiya eshintsha kahle ngokuhlanza nangokuthambisa ama-derailleur nokulungisa ukuxinana kwezintambo.
  6. Ukuhlolwa kwamabhodi: Hlola njalo amabhodi ku-handlebars, esiqwini, endaweni yesihlalo, nasemasondweni ukuze uqinisekise ukuthi aqinile futhi aphephile.
  7. Ukugcinwa kwamasondo: Hlola amasondo ukuze ubone ukuqina nokuxinana kwezikhulumi. Uma kudingeka, qinisa amasondo noma ubeke izikhulumi ezonakele esikhundleni sazo.
  8. Ukubukezwa njalo: Cabanga ngokulungiswa kochwepheshe minyaka yonke noma kaningi kuye ngokusetshenziswa ukuze ubhekane nokuguguleka kwezingxenye ezifana nama-bearings nezintambo.
Ngokulandela lezi zindlela ezibalulekile zokugcina, ungaqinisekisa ukuthi ibhayisikili lakho lihlala liphephile, lisebenza kahle, futhi limnandi ukuligibela.
